Quickunder is an automated Facultative Underwriter for a reinsurance company. 
It uses a combination of OpeanAI LLM and rule-based systems to assess risks and make underwriting decisions. The system is designed to streamline the underwriting process, reduce manual effort, and improve accuracy in risk assessment.
